All of my games are developed using the Godot Engine, which I enjoy working with for its flexibility, open-source philosophy, and strong support for 2D development. I’m especially passionate about creating 2D roguelike games, as I love designing replayable experiences with unique mechanics, randomized elements, and challenging progression systems. While roguelikes are my current focus, I plan to continue expanding my skills and building new titles over time. In the future, I’m also interested in exploring Metroidvania-style and platforming games, experimenting with world-building, movement systems, and interconnected level design to bring even more depth and variety to my projects.
